8. How to rename or change your name in PUBG Mobile?

Follow the mentioned steps below to rename or change your name in PUBG Mobile:

  1. Open 'PUBG Mobile' on your device.
  2. At the bottom right side, you will see an 'Inventory Option'then click on it.
  3. Click on the 'Crate Icon' at the bottom, and you will see a Rename Card.
  4. Click on the 'Rename Card' to rename or change your name.
  5. Once you have renamed or changed your name, click 'OK' to save changes.
